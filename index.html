Career opportunities in Germany

Are you interested in a career as a nurse in Germany? If yes, you came to the right place. Germany is in dire need of health care professionals. This is your chance to take the step and receive the support you need to get through the process.

Why healthcare is the best way to come to Germany and offers amazing opportunities also in future.

The life expectancy in Germany rises constantly but birth rates have been and continue decreasing. The result - an increasingly older society that needs care when they cannot take care of themself anymore.

Not enough young people in Germany are found to do the work as a nurse. Therefore many companies are open hiring from outside. Once you have finished a three year training, you can basically choose where you want to work - all doors are open within the health care sector.

You might ask yourself, do I need to pay for the training? The answer is no - but you are being paid during the training, enough to cover. your costs of living during the training period.

What's the catch?

Well, the bureaucratic steps in finding a place and getting all the steps approved plus the visa are tricky. You should have a person in Germany who can respond to certain inquiries of the different bodies involved in the process and someone who knows what steps to take when to get all requirements together. Also finding an apartment in Germany from abroad is rather difficult.

We are helping you with the entire process from your first interview with your future employer to settling in Germany. The service to you is free. All we need is your effort to meet what is required:

  • B2-German certificate
  • Highschool certificate
  • A valid passport
  • Your active participation in the process
